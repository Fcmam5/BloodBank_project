    About BloodBank project

    Simply is a tiny blood donation management system, which we tried to make blood transfusion procedures easier and quick by listing the blood stock's status, and list avalible donors.

    The procedures explained:
    • As a volunteer, the doctor can validate whether you can donate or not by our program's help.
    • Then you'll pass to a nurse which will fill more information into our system after the transfusion.
      If you were motivated to blood donation she/he'll keep your contact information to call you when they need you.
    • Then a laboratory will analyse your blood and send it to the bank if there were no problems, if it wasn't they will inform you about it.
    • Now the blood arrives to the BloodBank, where we can find it stock, a list of donors.
    • If a patient needs bloods, the doctor will send a request to the bank. They'll check the stock, and if they didn't find they'll call the avalible donors. If there were not they'll call other blood banks out of the region.
    We believe that that system can make some change later ｡◕‿◕｡ Inchaa Allah.
